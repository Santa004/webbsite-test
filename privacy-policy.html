Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. At Edtivo Energy, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share data. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and company details you provide through forms or account registration.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device type, and usage data automatically collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Transactional Information: If you purchase products or services, we collect payment and billing information securely.
How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our products and services

  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests

  • Send updates, newsletters, or promotional offers (with consent)

  • Monitor and analyze website usage for performance improvements

  • Ensure legal compliance and prevent fraud

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Trusted service providers who assist with hosting, payment processing, or email delivery

  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights

  • Business partners only with explicit consent

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience, improve website performance, and collect anonymous analytics data. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data

  • Request corrections or deletions

  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@airenergy.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
